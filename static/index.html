Gridfinity Label Generator

  1. Click Add Label to create a label
  2. Set Width in Gridfinity units (u) or mm
  3. Type your content — or pick a symbol from the Fragment Picker →
  4. Click Preview 3D to see a 3D render (auto-saved on switch)
  5. Click Download All at the bottom to export for printing

Tip: drag labels in the list to reorder · drag the divider to resize panels

Import / Export JSON in the header to save or restore your session
